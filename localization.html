FS-UAE Translations

FS-UAE and FS-UAE Launcher can be translated to other languages (than the default English), and the translations are included with every FS-UAE download. You can follow this link in order to view or edit translations:

https://crowdin.com/project/fs-uae.

If you want to create and maintain a translation for a another language, please contact me by e-mail or post a message to on this forum thread.
