default: 70
example: 100
since: 2.3.12
type: choice
---

Controls the amount of mixing of the stereo channels. 100% means that
the left and right channels are not mixed together at all, and 0% means
that you get mono sound output on stereo speaker.

Value: 100 (100%)
Value: 90 (90%)
Value: 80 (80%)
Value: 70 (70%)
Value: 60 (60%)
Value: 50 (50%)
Value: 40 (40%)
Value: 30 (30%)
Value: 20 (20%)
Value: 10 (10%)
Value: 0 (0%)
